Overview

The Three Pillars

Pure Logic

Logic is the skeleton of persuasion. Draft your outline in Plain Text. No boxes to drag, no fonts to fix, just your reasoning, organized and powerful.

Tactile Intuition

Intuition is the soul of design. Sculpt your visuals on a fluid canvas. Direct manipulation, guided by a silent AI that respects the geometry of beauty.

Infinite Safety

Memory is the foundation of growth. Every moment is preserved in a living Time Capsule, so yesterday's idea is never lost when today's direction changes.
